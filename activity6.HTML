A Part of Health and Hygiene | 18-06-2025 to 23-06-2025

Community Health Survey

We noticed many people suffer from health problems due to addiction to street foods. We explained the risks and supplied medicines and face masks to the elderly population in the area.

Promoting Healthy Eating

Eating fruits is highly beneficial. We educated the community on their value, emphasizing these key points:

  • Nutrient-Rich: Provide vitamins, potassium, and fiber.
  • Hydration: High water content helps maintain hydration.
  • Disease Prevention: Antioxidants reduce the risk of chronic diseases.
  • Digestive Health: Fiber promotes healthy digestion.
